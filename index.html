About Me

Hello! I am a Senior at James Madison University studying Computer Science with a minor in Communication Studies. This past summer I intetened with Accenture Federal Services in Washington, DC. where I expanded on my programming skills, learned R and D3, and even dabbled a little with AWS. I also, along with a group of 3 other interns, created a dashboard of data on immigrants and refugees over the course of the last 20 years. I really enjoyed learning new skills and getting a glimpse into a future in consulting, as I will be working there again after graduation as a Software Engineer!

Currently I'm interested in the front end development side of things and figuring out the best way to effectively communicate ideas and progress between programmers and clients.

I'm passionate about educating women, especially in STEM related fields, and getting young girls (and boys, and everyone) exposed to things like programming early on.

I was born and raised just outside of Richmond, VA and have lived there my whole life. Outside of my life behind a computer screen I enjoy cooking, Netflixing, hiking, talking, and just spending time with my friends and family. During the summer I LOVE going to my family's little cabin on Branch Lake in Ellsworth, Maine. Before joining the real world, I was a camp counselor at an overnight camp up there, Camp Jordan, for several years, and am very passionate about Maine and Summer Camp in general.
